In the Media#

Faurecia in Conversation (Ep. 1): Discussing Active Noise Control solutions and the future of mobility as General Manager of Faurecia Creo Dynamics.

In this debut episode of the Faurecia in Conversation podcast, Jean-François Rondeau discusses the role of Faurecia Creo Dynamics in shaping the future of acoustic comfort.

  • Active Noise Control (ANC): How digital solutions replace heavy sound-deadening materials to reduce weight and CO2 emissions.
  • EOC vs. RNC: The technical distinction between Engine Order Control (canceling engine boom) and Road Noise Control (essential for the silent cabins of Electric Vehicles).
  • Creo Dynamics Heritage: Leveraging expertise from the aerospace sector to solve complex automotive noise challenges.
